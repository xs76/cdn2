Aquapark.io

In Aquapark.io you compete with other players in a race that takes place on a gigantic water park slide. It goes on and on, seemingly spiraling into infinity, but there is an end and the first person to reach the finish line wins. It is not easy to stay on track and there’s always a danger of getting thrown off the slide by someone else, but once you get the hang of the gameplay you will be able to push the others off and take shortcuts by jumping off on your own and landing somewhere on a lower level.

How to play Aquapark.io

Control your character by holding the mouse button and steering in the appropriate direction. Steer harder to jump off and keep moving to stick the perfect landing. Can you master the game and become the best racer on the world’s greatest water slide? Give Aquapark.io a try and find out!
